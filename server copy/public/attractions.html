Space Center Houston

The Space Center Houston is a must-visit destination for space enthusiasts and curious minds alike. As the official visitor center for NASA's Johnson Space Center, it offers an immersive experience into the world of space exploration. Visitors can get up close to real spacecraft, including the famous Saturn V rocket, and explore interactive exhibits that showcase humanity’s journey to the stars. From behind-the-scenes tours of NASA’s Mission Control to hands-on activities for all ages, the Space Center provides a unique opportunity to learn about the past, present, and future of space missions. It's a place where the wonders of space become a reality, inspiring explorers of all ages!

Houston Zoo

The Houston Zoo is one of the city's most beloved attractions, offering a unique and immersive experience for animal lovers of all ages. Home to over 6,000 animals representing more than 600 species, the zoo provides visitors with the chance to connect with wildlife from around the world. Located within the lush Hermann Park, the Houston Zoo features beautifully designed habitats, from the African Forest to the Kipp Aquarium, and offers engaging, educational programs and conservation efforts. Whether you're admiring majestic elephants, playful primates, or exotic birds, the Houston Zoo promises an unforgettable day of adventure and learning in the heart of the city.

Houston Museum of Natural Science

The Houston Museum of Natural Science is a premier cultural and educational destination, offering a fascinating journey through time, nature, and science. Known for its stunning exhibits, the museum features everything from towering dinosaur skeletons in the Hall of Paleontology to dazzling gems and minerals in the Cullen Hall of Gems and Minerals. Visitors can explore ancient civilizations, marvel at the wonders of space in the Burke Baker Planetarium, and even experience the beauty of live butterflies in the Cockrell Butterfly Center. With rotating special exhibits and engaging hands-on displays, the Houston Museum of Natural Science inspires curiosity and discovery for all ages.
